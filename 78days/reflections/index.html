Beyond the archive.

This winter, student researchers at the Starling Lab examined the 78 Days Archive and considered its implications on the future of journalism and beyond.

Their thoughtful reflections open many important conversations that lie ahead. While each are unique, the threads that binds them are the values embedded in the Starling Framework and the responsibility that the next generation of journalists and technologists have to ensure their success.
